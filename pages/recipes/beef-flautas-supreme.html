Beef Flautas Supreme

with Pico de Gallo & Smoky Red Pepper Crema

Ingredients

  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 10 oz Ground Beef
  • 1 TBSP Southwest Spice Blend
  • 2 TBSP Tex-Mex Paste
  • 6 Flour Tortillas
  • 1/2 Cup Mexican Cheese Blend
  • 1 Tomato
  • 4 TBSP Smoky Red Pepper Crema

Steps

  1. Adjust the rack to middle position and preheat oven to 425 degrees. Line a baking sheet with foil and brush with oil. Wash and dry produce.

    Halve, peel, and thinly slice onion; mince a few slices until you have 1 TBSP.

  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add sliced onion; cook for 3 minutes.

    Add beef and Southwest Spice Blend; cook, breaking up meat into pieces, until beef is browned and onion is softened, 4-6 minutes.

    Stir in Tex-Mex paste and 1/2 cup water. Simmer until mixture has thickened and beef is cooked through, 2-4 minutes more. Turn off heat.

  3. Meanwhile, drizzle tortillas with 1 TBSP olive oil; brush or rub completely to coat.

    Place tortillas on a clean work surface. Once beef filling is done, add a heaping 1/2 cup to one side of each tortilla, then sprinkle each with 1 TBSP Mexican cheese blend. Roll up tortillas, starting with filled sides, to create flautas. Place seam sides down, on prepared sheet.

    Bake on middle rack until golden brown and crispy, 8-12 minutes.

  4. While flautas bake, finely dice tomato. In a small bowl, combine tomamto and minced onion. Season with salt.

    Divide flautas between plates. Top with pico de gallo and red pepper crema. Serve.
